⚡ BetterLife — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BetterLife ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • App data you create: habits, journal entries, health logs, finance data, mood logs, work sessions, goals

We do not collect: your name, phone number, location, contacts, or any device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and sync the BetterLife service across your devices
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never use your data for advertising

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU region. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

4. Journal Privacy

Journal entries are protected by a 4-digit PIN you set on your device. The PIN is stored as a one-way hash — we cannot read your journal entries.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete Account).

6. Children's Privacy

BetterLife is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Google Play — app distribution

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (available in the app)
  • Export your data (Settings → Export backup)
  • Delete your account and all data
